Software Development Agreement

1. Project Overview

This Software Development Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into between the Client and the Development Team for the creation of a comprehensive software solution. The project scope includes full-stack development, user interface design, and system integration services.

2. Scope of Work

The development team will provide complete software development services including requirements analysis, system design, coding, testing, and deployment. All work will be performed according to industry best practices and standards.

3. Timeline and Milestones

The project is scheduled to be completed within 12 weeks from the start date. Key milestones include initial design approval, prototype delivery, beta testing, and final deployment.

4. Payment Terms

Payment will be made according to the agreed schedule with 30% upfront, 40% at milestone completion, and 30% upon final delivery. All payments are due within 30 days of invoice receipt.

5. Intellectual Property

All custom code and designs created specifically for this project will be owned by the Client upon final payment. Third-party components and libraries remain under their respective licenses.

6. Quality Assurance

The development team will conduct thorough testing including unit tests, integration tests, and user acceptance testing. All deliverables will meet agreed-upon quality standards.

7. Communication

Regular progress updates will be provided weekly through status reports and monthly progress meetings. All communication will be documented and tracked.

8. Change Management

Any changes to the original scope will be documented through formal change requests and require approval from both parties before implementation.

9. Support and Maintenance

The development team will provide 90 days of bug fixes and technical support following the final delivery. Extended support packages are available separately.

10. Confidentiality

Both parties agree to maintain strict confidentiality regarding all project information, proprietary data, and business processes shared during the development process.

11. Termination

Either party may terminate this agreement with 30 days written notice. Upon termination, all work completed to date will be delivered and payment made for services rendered.

12. Liability

The development team's liability is limited to the total contract value. Neither party will be liable for indirect, special, or consequential damages.

13. Force Majeure

Neither party will be liable for delays caused by circumstances beyond their reasonable control, including natural disasters, government actions, or other unforeseeable events.

14. Governing Law

This agreement will be governed by the laws of the applicable jurisdiction and any disputes will be resolved through binding arbitration.

15. Signatures

By signing below, both parties acknowledge they have read, understood, and agree to be bound by the terms and conditions of this Software Development Agreement.
